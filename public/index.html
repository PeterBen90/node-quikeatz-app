Recipe Storage Platform

Quik Eatz is a recipe storage platform that allows each user to create and post food and/or cocktail recipes. Storing and tracking recipes can be messy when dealing with multiple cookbooks, but with Quik Eatz you can store all your favourtie recipes in one place - to help with you daily dietary and meal plans!

Store and Manage Recipes

Quik Eatz features a user-friendly interface that allows you to add recipe desciptions to your account. Once you've added your recipe, it will be stored in your dashboard for you to edit or delete whenver you choose.
