Recipe Sharing Platform

Quik Eatz is a recipe sharing platform that allows each user to create and post food and/or cocktail recipes. Quik eats allows users to rate and save recipes posted by other users for your own personal online cookbook. Register and you'll have access to new and fun recipes to try any time you choose!
